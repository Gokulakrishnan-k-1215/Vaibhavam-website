About Vaibhavam

Fresh food, traditional flavours and warm hospitality.

Good Food. Comfortable Place.

Vaibhavam is a family-friendly hotel and coffee shop in Polur. We serve freshly prepared coffee, tea, snacks, juices and traditional meals.


Our location offers convenient parking and is suitable for local families, hospital visitors, travellers and pilgrims.
